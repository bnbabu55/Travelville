2.5. Bookings held without payment are classed as unconfirmed and may be cancelled at any time

2.6. Clients wishing to pay by BAC’s must do so immediately upon receiving the pro-forma invoice; and provide a credit or debit card to hold against the booking

3.2. Cancellation of any converted booking (excluding group reservations) is as follows: 72 hours for less than 27 nights, 7 days for 28-90 nights & 14 days for more than 91 nights.

3.3. If a Client or their guest wishes to depart earlier than the reserved departure date they must give at least the above amount of notice, depending on the length of their stay; otherwise no refund will be given.

3.5. If a cancelled or amended booking was booked under a promotional rate or code no refunds will be given, unless the rate terms are stated otherwise

Travelville will endeavour to grant any requests for such extension, however, this will be strictly subject to availability and cannot be guaranteed. An alternative apartment may be offered if the apartment that was initially booked is unavailable for all or any part of the extension period.

3.7. These terms and conditions apply to any extension of the booking as they do to the original booking.
